iAnalytix

Welcome! iAnalytix. is a free real time web analytics system which allows you to track the source your visitors are coming from, such as search engines, including your keywords, referring sites and you may also get a report on your most popular pages on you website.

Why iAnalytix?

Our aim was to create a fast and reliable system that would provide you with real time statistics, meaning that in the moment a visitor arrives to your website, its "footprints" are visible in the statistics of your webpage.
